FAQ

Common questions answered.

Do I need to change my business phone number?

No. You keep your existing number. You simply set up a call-forward in your carrier's settings to route calls to your myAIvoiceStudio virtual number. We provide step-by-step instructions per carrier during onboarding.

What happens if the agent doesn't know an answer?

The agent is designed to never fabricate answers. If the information isn't in your knowledge base, it will tell the caller it doesn't know and offer to connect them to a human or provide your direct line.

Can the agent handle multiple appointment types?

Yes. You define each service (e.g., "60-minute Deep Tissue" or "30-minute Consultation") during setup. The agent automatically applies the correct duration when booking and will ask if the caller is unsure.

What if two customers call for the same slot simultaneously?

Our Soft-Lock mechanism temporarily reserves a slot the moment a caller shows interest. This prevents double-bookings during live conversations. Conflict events are logged in your dashboard as "Lost Opportunities."

Is my customers' data safe? What about recording consent?

All call recordings and transcripts are encrypted and isolated per account. A consent notification is played at the start of each call. Customer names and phone numbers are stored in encrypted form, never in plain text.

Can I turn the agent off temporarily?

Absolutely. A single toggle in your dashboard deactivates the agent instantly. Calls will fall through to your original number. Any active call in progress is allowed to complete before the toggle takes effect.

How is the $0.05/min AI cost calculated?

The AI cost covers speech recognition, language model processing, and text-to-speech for the duration of each call. A typical 3-minute booking call costs roughly $0.15. Your monthly bill is your subscription plus total AI minutes used.

What's included in the $249 setup fee?

The one-time setup fee covers dedicated virtual number provisioning, guided onboarding support, initial knowledge base configuration assistance, and integration verification with your Google Calendar and phone carrier.
